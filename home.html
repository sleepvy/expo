Storm a Brewin'

Hello!

Welcome to Storm a Brewin'. In this AR (Augmented Reality) game, you will be sent on a wild adventure!

You were wandering in the wicked woods of the ATLAS building, and stumbled upon the little witch, [name]. Finals are coming up fast, and she needs your help to ace her exams! She enlisted you to help her collect some rare ingredients to brew up a certain-A potion.

You need to collect:

  • A puddle of tears
  • A shot of coffee
  • An ounce of hope
  • One Ch(egg)
  • A dash of exhaustion

Look around the ATLS Expo to find these items! You will know you have found an item when you see a paper QR tag like this: Point your phone at it and see what you have found! Then, collect the item by clicking it.

Best of luck helping the little witch pass her exams (and good luck on your own)! Enjoy the adventure.
