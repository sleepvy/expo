Welcome to Storm a Brewin'.

You were wandering in the wicked woods of the ATLAS building, and stumbled upon the little witch. She needs your help to ace her exams!

You need to collect:

  • A puddle of tears
  • A shot of coffee
  • An ounce of hope
  • One Ch(egg)
  • A dash of exhaustion

Look around the ATLS Expo to find these items! You will know you have found an item when you see a paper marker. Point your phone at it and see what you have found! Collect the item by clicking it.
